504 Banks of the Sacramento

transpose
font size

This sea shanty was originally written by the Hutchinson Family Singers in 1849 to fit a different tune. But the next year the very popular “Camptown Races” song was published by Stephen Foster. Not long after this song merged to fit Foster’s catchy tune. It spread far and wide over sea and land. It even got a German version “De Hamborger Veermaster,” which retained the music and the English chorus.
