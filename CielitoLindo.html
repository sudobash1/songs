This folksong was written in 1882 by Quirino Mendoza y Cortés, and has become an iconic song of Mexico.

The Sierra Morena mountains of Mexico were occupied by bandits in the 17ᵗʰ century, and it entered into the folksong tradition of Mexico. In this song it has become romanticised as the woman’s eyes are like the bandits coming down from the mountain.
