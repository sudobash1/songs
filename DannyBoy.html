103 Danny Boy

transpose
font size

This famous song is deeply associated with Ireland, but it was actually written by Frederic Weatherly, an English lawyer, in 1913. He had originally written these lyrics to another melody, but he changed this after he was sent a copy of the traditional Irish tune “Londonderry Air.”

The meaning behind the song is the subject of much speculation. It is typically thought that the song is a parent’s farewell to their child who is leaving either because of war or the Irish Potato Famine.
