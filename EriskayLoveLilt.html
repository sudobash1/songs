Eriskay is a tiny island in the Scottish Hebrides of less than 3 square miles. It was collected and translated from Scots Gaelic by Marjory Kennedy-Fraser, who did much work, collecting and preserving Hebridean folksongs.

The refrain is a lilt, consisting of expressive syllables without any meaning. In the lilting, “bh” is pronounced as “v” and “ei” as “ay”
