Sir Charles Trevelyan was responsible for administrating relief during the Irish Potato Famine. However, he was a devotee of absolute capitalism, and rejected any suggestion of reducing the export of food out of Ireland during the famine. He wrote that the famine was God’s judgement on the Irish people and a means of culling surplus population.

At the time in Ireland, criminals could be deported to Botany Bay, which was a penal colony in Australia. This song’s protagonist has suffered this fate for theft from the Government’s food stores.
