301 Scarborough Fair

transpose
font size

This folksong is descended from a 16ᵗʰ century broadside called the Elfin Knight. It acquired the refrain about herbs in the 18ᵗʰ century, and later the location of Scarborough Fair. Originally, it was a humorous song, sung quickly, but in modern times it was slowed down and made much more serious.
