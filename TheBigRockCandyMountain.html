Harry McClintock, the famous hobo singer, first recorded this song in 1928. He claimed to have written it as a homeless youth as a street singer.

This bright song about a hobo’s idea of paradise, has a darker side too. The original, unpublished song from McClintock’s street performance has another verse. It made it clear that the singer is a hobo trying to convince a homeless youth to work for him with tales of this imaginary paradise.
