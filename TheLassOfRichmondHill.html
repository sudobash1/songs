This quintessential British song was actually written by an Irish revolutionary, Leonard McNally. Although McNally helped found the United Irishmen, he turned informant to the British a few years later for profit and immunity.

McNally did marry his “lass of Richmond Hill;” however, she died in childbirth not long afterwards.
