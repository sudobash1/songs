16 The Rising of the Moon

font size

This song is about the Irish Rebellion of 1798. It dates back to the middle of the 19ᵗʰ century, but the tune is much older. The words were written by John Keegan Casey.

It is not known if Shawn O’Farrell was a real figure. The name does not correspond to any known revolutionary figure.
