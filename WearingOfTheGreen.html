112 The Wearing of the Green

font size

This song is about the aftermath of the Irish Rebellion of 1798. The revolutionaries had taken green as their color, and after the failed rebellion against the British, wearing green clothing could be seen as an act of rebellion.

The man “Napper Tandy” referenced in the second verse was an Irish rebel leader who was exiled into France.
